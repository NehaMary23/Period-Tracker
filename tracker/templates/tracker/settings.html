ℹ️ Information
Understanding Your Cycle
  • Menstrual Phase: First 5-7 days of your cycle. Characterized by bleeding.
  • Follicular Phase: Days 1-13. Hormone levels rise, preparing for ovulation.
  • Ovulation: Day 14 (approximate). An egg is released.
  • Luteal Phase: Days 15-28. Progesterone rises, preparing the uterus for a possible pregnancy.
Common Symptoms

Track symptoms to identify patterns in your cycle:
